Instructions

Welcome to the Pig Game! The objective is simple: Get the score to 100 first by rolling the dice. However, you can only lock in your current score by pressing "Hold". This will move the score from the current score into your actual score and start the next players turn. If you manage to roll a 1, all of the points you have not locked in are gone and it is the next plyers turn.
